## ---=== Character Database Commands ===---
## `/check_oc`
This command **lists the specifics** of the character selected.

Parameters:
- ocindex: `Type: String.` This will be the **OC Index** of your character. If you don't know it, just run `/oc_list`!
- OPTIONAL userid: `Type: String.` This will be the **User ID** of the person you'll want to see the list of. If it's left blank, the ID will be of **the one who runs the command**.
