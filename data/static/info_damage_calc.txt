## ---=== Battle Commands ===---
## `/damage_calc`
This command calculates **approximate damage** with a few numbers.

Parameters:
- movepower: `Type: Int.` This is the **Base Power** of the move used.
- selfatkstat: `Type: Int.` This is the **Corresponding Offense Stat** of the Pokemon **using** the move.
- foedefstat: `Type: Int.` This is the **Corresponding Defense Stat** of the Pokemon **taking** the damage.
- OPTIONAL level: `Type: Int.` This is the **Level** of the Pokemon **using** the move. Defaults to 100.
