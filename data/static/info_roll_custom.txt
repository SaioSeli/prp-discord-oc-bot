## ---=== Fun Commands ===---
## `/roll_custom`
This is a (better, objectively,) dice roll simulator.

Parameters:
- min: `Type: Int.` This is the **lowest number the dice can reach**.
- max: `Type: Int.` This is the **highest number the dice can reach**.
- rolls: `Type: Int.` This is the amount of **dices that are rolled**.
